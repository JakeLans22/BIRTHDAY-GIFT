HOW TO PERSONALIZE YOUR SCRAPBOOK
==================================

1. PHOTOS
   Drop your own photos into the "images/photos" folder, named:
     photo1.jpg, photo2.jpg, photo3.jpg, photo4.jpg, photo5.jpg, photo6.jpg
   The polaroid frames will pick them up automatically. If a photo is
   missing, the frame just shows a soft gradient placeholder — nothing breaks.

2. MUSIC
   Add your favorite love song to the "audio" folder as:
     audio/our-song.mp3
   Update the song title/artist text in the "Music Corner" section of
   index.html.

3. COUNTDOWN
   Open script.js and edit this line near the top with the date you got
   together:
     const RELATIONSHIP_START = new Date('2022-06-14T00:00:00');

4. WORDS
   Everything in "Our Story", "Love Letters", "Reasons I Love You",
   "Special Moments", and "Future Dreams" is placeholder text written to
   match the mood — swap it out for your real memories in index.html.

That's it — open index.html in any browser to view it.
